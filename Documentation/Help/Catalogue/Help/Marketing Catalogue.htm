Marketing Catalogue:


This form generates the same order form as it generates in the “Catalogue Order” menu. But this form has more search
criteria with the various options.
  • Enter Quotation No and Customer Name, select Jewel Description, and enter From and To weight.
  • There are various options available for Diamond Wt as shown below.

  • Click on “Jewel” button, it will show below form, with all the “Necklases” of Net Wt between 1 and 100.
  • Select the jewels on left hand side checkbox. If there are more than 10 records, it will be available in subsequent pages.
  • After selecting jewels, click on “Home” button, it will redirect to main form with selected records.
  • Click on “Process” button, to generate the order
  • There are Advanced Filter options available, for more refined search with various weight combinations.
  • Click on “Ok” button, then “Jewel” button and follow subsequent steps to generate marketing order.
